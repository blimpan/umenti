# Metis Design Specification

## Visual Language

- **Audience:** Students aged 14–17 (initial beachhead), teachers
- **Tone:** Friendly, focused, rewarding. Duolingo-inspired energy without being childish.
- **Mode:** Light mode first. Dark mode planned for future.
- **Accent color:** Teal
- **Font:** [Nunito](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Nunito) (Google Fonts) — rounded sans-serif, balances approachability with readability
- **Theme switching:** Future feature — students may be able to choose their own theme

---

## App Structure

Single unified app with role-based views (student vs. teacher). Shared shell, role-specific routes and layouts.
